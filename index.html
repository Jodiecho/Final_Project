About Insomnia Cookies

Insomnia Cookies started in 2003 when founder Seth Berkowitz, craving warm, sweet treats late at night, created a business to satisfy those needs. It began in his dorm room and grew into a company that revolutionized late-night food delivery. Since then, they've opened stores, launched nationwide shipping, and added new items like vegan cookies and Dippers. Today, Insomnia Cookies continues to serve sweet treats to late-night fans with over 200 stores and a flagship CookieLab in Philadelphia.
